✿ the signature treat

brown butter
cookies

crinkled tops, gooey middles ♡
✦ small-batch only ♡ local pickup ✿ made to order

the butter goes in the pan first. it spits, foams, turns amber — that's when it gets good. mixed by hand into a dough that's half cookie, half caramel memory, then packed with chocolate chunks so generously it's almost rude.

edges crisp, middles soft, finished with a pinch of sea salt. the kind of cookie you eat warm, standing in the kitchen, before they've had a chance to cool.
